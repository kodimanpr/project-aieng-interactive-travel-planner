It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2009 and is located at Puerto Rico Highway 798 , Km. 1.0 in Río Cañas barrio.  \\n\nIt has also been known as Bridge #6, as Ponton La Concepcion, and as (La) Concepcion Bridge.   It spans the Cañas River .  It is a brick barrel vault bridge.  It was built as part of the Río Piedras-Caguas highway program.   \\n\nThe image at right is from Our Islands and their People by Jose de Olivares, published in 1899 after the Spanish-American War.\\n\nReferences\n\\n\nThis article about a property in Puerto Rico on the National Register of Historic Places is a stub .
