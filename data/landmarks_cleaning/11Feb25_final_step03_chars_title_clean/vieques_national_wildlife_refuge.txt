It is part of the Caribbean Islands National Wildlife Refuge Complex .\\n\nThe refuge contains several ecologically distinct habitats including; beaches, coastal lagoons , mangrove wetlands , and upland forested areas. Some of the best examples of sub-tropical dry forest in the Caribbean can be found on refuge lands.\\n\nThe marine environment surrounding the refuge contains coral reefs and sea grass beds.
