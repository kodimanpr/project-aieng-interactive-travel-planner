It is located in Old San Juan , San Juan , Puerto Rico .  The school was founded in 1965 as part of the Institute of Puerto Rican Culture . Painter José Antonio Torres Martinó was one of the school\\'s co-founders.  The school\\'s first director was Miguel Pou .  \\n\nAs an autonomous school it was created by an amendment of legislation by the Legislative Assembly of Puerto Rico in 1971, and achieved its definitive form and autonomy under Public Law 54 of August 22, 1990.\\n\nThe school offers bachelor degrees in seven concentrations: graphic arts , photography and design (with specialties in digital graphic design and photography and motion), art education , sculpture , painting , industrial design and fashion design . Today, the school is Puerto Rico\\'s foremost institution of higher education in the visual arts.
