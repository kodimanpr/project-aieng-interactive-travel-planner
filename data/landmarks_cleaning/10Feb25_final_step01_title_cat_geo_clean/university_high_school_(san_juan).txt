UHS students have breakfast and lunch at the Lunchroom belonging to the University Elementary School, located next to the UHS.\\n\nStudent organizations\nUHS has a student council alongside several organized clubs such as a Spanish Oratory club, a Model United Nations club, an English Forensics League Club, a National Honors Society chapter, among others.\\n\nSports\nUHS has several sports teams, including a soccer team, a volleyball team, swimming team and a basketball team.\\n\n2017 University of Puerto Rico strikes\nThe University High School joined the University of Puerto Rico strikes on March 28, 2017.
