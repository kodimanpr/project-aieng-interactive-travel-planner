Along with Palominos island , it is part of the La Cordillera Reef Nature Reserve and under the jurisdiction of the Department of Natural Resources and Environment . Icacos is a fifteen-minute water taxi ride from Fajardo.  The cay is a popular snorkeling and beach tourism destination.\\n\nFor some time, there was a limestone quarry on the southern part of the island, with a short railroad system to shuttle limestone from the quarry to the pier.  \\n\nSee also\nReferences\nExternal links\n\\n\nThis Fajardo, Puerto Rico location article is a stub .
