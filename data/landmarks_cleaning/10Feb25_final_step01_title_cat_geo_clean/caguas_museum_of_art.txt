The first level of the museum is dedicated to Caguas native painter Carlos Osorio and the second level offers temporary exhibitions. The entrance is free and guided tours are available.  The museum also host cultural events.\\n\nCasa Amarilla\nThe museum is located in a historic two-story, neoclassical building called Casa Amarilla (Spanish for \\'yellow house) which dates to the early 20th century. It is located on the corner of Padial and Ruiz Belvis streets.
